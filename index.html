Important Safety Disclaimer

This tool displays real-time data from USGS (U.S. Geological Survey) water monitoring stations. While we strive to display accurate information, data may be delayed, incomplete, or temporarily unavailable.

Always verify current conditions through official sources before entering any body of water. River and lake conditions can change rapidly and without warning.

Flood stage indicators are for reference only and do not constitute official flood warnings. For official warnings contact the National Weather Service at weather.gov.
